# Make a Cup of Coffee (Algorithm)

## Inputs
- Cup
- Water
- Coffee granules

## Optional Inputs
- Milk
- Sugar

## Steps
1. Fill kettle with water
2. Heat water until boiling
3. Add coffee granules to cup
4. Pour hot water into cup
5. (Optional) Add milk or sugar
6. Stir the coffee
7. Let the coffee cool
8. Drink

## Output
A cup of coffee

---

## Notes
- This is a procedural algorithm (step-by-step execution)
- Each step depends on the previous one
- Can be translated into functions in code
